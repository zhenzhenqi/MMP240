Technical Guide

Pen Tool, Direct Selection Tool, and Pathfinders

Hold OPTION key and drag a shape allows you to reproduce it.

The last option in PEN TOOL's dropdown menu allows you to convert between sharp and rounded points.

Pathfinders allow you to combine and subtract shapes.

File==>Document Setup==>Edit Artboards to adjust size of Artboards.

Select a Typography Dialogue box, go to Type==>Create Outline will covert selected typography to shape. You can then edit it as a regular shape in Illustrator, but you won't be able to perform Character operations on them anymore.
