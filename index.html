Welcome to COMP104, Responsive Design. Together, we will create a fully responsive, styled, and interactive website in 15 weeks. We will also talk about User Experience related topics such as design and empathy, immersive experience design, interaction design, and critical play. We will also discuss web design in relation to art and technology; Web design under the context of new media and maker movement, and what it means to be a web designer and digital content creator in the age of new media.

Email me at zhenzhenqi@gmail.com any time for questions. Please write COMP104 on the title of the email.
